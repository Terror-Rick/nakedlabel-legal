Privacy Policy

App: NakedLabel
Developer: NakedLabel Support
Effective Date: March 27, 2026

NakedLabel helps you make informed choices about the products you buy by scanning barcodes and analyzing ingredient lists. The short version: NakedLabel does not sell your data, does not store photos, and does not collect your location. Most data stays on your device.

1. Camera Access

NakedLabel requests access to your device camera solely to scan barcodes. No photos or images are captured, stored, or transmitted.

2. Account and Identity

NakedLabel uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication — a randomly assigned anonymous ID with no connection to your name or email. You may optionally sign in with Google for premium features. You are never required to create an account.

3. Scan History and Product Data

Barcodes and grading results are stored locally on your device and in Firebase Firestore under your anonymous UID for fast repeat scans. You can delete your scan history at any time from within the app.

4. Ingredient Analysis

Ingredient names are sent to Google Gemini AI via secure Firebase Cloud Functions for health grading. No personal information is included in these requests.

5. In-App Purchases

RevenueCat manages subscriptions and purchase receipts. NakedLabel receives only your subscription status — never your payment details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

6. Analytics and Crash Reporting

Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collect anonymous, aggregated usage and crash data to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included.

7. What We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Photos or images
  • Contacts or messages
  • Payment card information

NakedLabel does not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

9. Data Security

All Firebase data is restricted by security rules to your authenticated UID. All transmissions are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

10. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete scan history: Available anytime in app settings.
  • Request data deletion: Email support@nakedlabel.org with your anonymous UID. We will delete your data within 30 days.
  • Google Sign-In users: Revoke access via Google Account Settings.

11. Children's Privacy

NakedLabel is not directed at children under 13. Contact support@nakedlabel.org to report any concerns.

12. Changes to This Policy

When changes are made, the Effective Date will be updated. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance.
